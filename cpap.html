C pap

Product Description

A continuous positive airway pressure (CPAP) machine is a medical device that delivers a constant, gentle flow of pressurized air into your nose or mouth to keep your airway open while you sleep.1 Healthcare providers prescribe CPAP therapy to treat sleep-related breathing disorders like obstructive sleep apnea, in which tissues in your mouth and throat narrow or close your airways, causing your breath to stop momentarily.
2 Using a CPAP machine can be a significant lifestyle change. You will need to use it every night, including when you travel, and likely during naps as well. However, it's an effective treatment option that can improve your sleep and your overall quality of life. There are several different CPAP options, and they're all relatively simple to use.
